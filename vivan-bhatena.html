    Vivan Bhatena is a film and TV actor, born on 28th October 1987, hailing from India and belongs to a Hindu family. He works in the Hindi cinema and entertainment industry, appeared in various movies and TV serials. The young actor is mainly known for the role in the serial “Kumkum”.

    Career

    Initially, Vivan Bhatena started his career as a model, and later, he moved into TV serials and films. He first appeared in the most popular drama serial “Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahoo Thi” had run from 2004-2005 in which he portrayed the role of Abhishek. Since then, he frequently worked in a variety of TV serials including “Kehna Hai Kuch Mujhko” which had run from 2004 to 2005.

    In 2005, he appeared in “Time Bomb 9/11”, and also made an appearance in the serial “Vaidehi” which had run in 2006.

    From 2006 to 2007, he appeared in the most popular serial “Kumkum”.  However, he has also worked in many serials including “Choona Hai Asmaan” which had run from 2007 to 2008, “Mayka”, “Chaand Per Chalo”, “Pyaar Ka Bandhan” and others are worth sharing.

    Apart from this, he has worked in various movies including “Judwaa 2”, released in 2017, “Dangal” released in 2016, “Raja The Great”, released in 2017, and others that are worth watching.

    However, he also appeared in a music video of famed singer Falguni Pathak “Maine Payal Hai Chankai”.

    Achievements

    Among notable achievements, he was awarded the title of Mr. India World title in 2001.
