    Hollie Strano was born in Lyndhurst, Cleveland, Ohio, on December 3, 1972. She is of American descent and of Caucasian descent. Strano grew up with her two siblings, Stephanie Strano and Mike Strano, in Lyndhurst, New Jersey.
