    Homa Bash Reporter

    Bash began her journalism career at CNN working as an intern at Election Center with Campbell Brown from June 2008 to September 2008. She then worked at KTVZ for two years as a news producer and reporter. In 2012, she joined KTAB/KRBC in Abilene, Texas Area where she worked as an anchor, producer, and reporter. From September 2016 to 2018, Bash was a reporter for NBC DFW.

    Homa Bash News Channel 5

    Bash joined News Channel 5 in 2014 and returned there in 2017. She works as an anchor and reporter alongside Danita Harris and Tracy Carloss.
