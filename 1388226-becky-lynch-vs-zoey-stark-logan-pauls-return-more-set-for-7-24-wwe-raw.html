    WWE is loading up the card for the July 24 episode of WWE RAW.

    As confirmed on the July 17 episode, Becky Lynch will have the chance to earn a rematch with Trish Stratus when she faces Zoey Stark. Stratus previously beat Lynch after Stark attacked “The Man” at WWE Night of Champions. Lynch has continued to feud with the duo ever since. If Lynch defeats Stark on July 24, she will earn another match with the WWE Hall of Famer.

    WWE also announced that Logan Paul will make his return on Monday. Paul previously appeared on the July 10 episode of WWE RAW and turned Ricochet down when the high-flying star challenged him to a match. Ricochet issued another challenge, as he told Paul to meet him on RAW, and the megastar accepted.

    Additionally, Drew McIntyre and WWE Intercontinental Champion Gunther will be come face-to-face. They have been feuding since McIntyre’s return to WWE at the Money in the Bank premium live event, and “The Scottish Warrior” has made it clear he wants the gold.

    The announced card for the July 24 episode of WWE RAW is as follows:

    • Becky Lynch vs. Zoey Stark
    • Drew McIntyre goes face-to-face with WWE Intercontinental Champion Gunther
    • Logan Paul returns

    WrestleZone will have full coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on July 24.
