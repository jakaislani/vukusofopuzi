    Wiki/Biography

    Suchi Purja was born on Friday, 11 November 1988 (age 33 years; as of 2021) in Nepal. She has her roots in Nepal and resides in Hampshire, England. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.

    Suchi Purja works as a Dental Hygienist/Therapist at Solutions Dental Clinic in Winchester, England. On 31 January 2020, she was appointed as one of the directors at Mountain Philantrophy Limited in Eastleigh, Hampshire, England.
