    08 May 2024, 00:00

    This just in Trainers – Timburr will be our next Community Day buddy!

    Taking place on October 15, 2023, from 2:00 p.m until 5:00 p.m. local time, Trainers will have the chance to catch Timburr, the Muscular Pokémon, which will be the highlighted Pokémon during the Community Day October 2023.

    If you decide to evolve Timburr during the event or up to five hours after the event ends, you will get a Conkeldurr with the Charged Attack Brutal Swing. Besides this, the bonuses that will be active during the event are the following:

    • 3x Stardust for catching Pokémon
    • 2x Candy for catching Pokémon
    • 2x chance for Trainers level 31 and up to receive Candy XL from catching Pokémon
    • Lure Modules will last for three hours
    • Incense will last for three hours (excluding Daily Adventure Incense)
    • Snapshots for a surprise
    • One additional Special Trade can be made for a maximum of two for the day (lasting up until 10:00 p.m.)
    • Trades will require 50% less Stardust (lasting up until 10:00 p.m.)

    Trainers can purchase a Community Day Special Research Story named Muscle Memories for $1. There will also be Community Day-themed Field Research tasks, with rewards such as encounters with Timburr, Great Balls, and more.

    Lasting up until 10:00 p.m. on October 15, 2023, Trainers can join special four-star Raid Battles, and the Pokémon that will be featured as a Raid Boss will be Gurdurr. Timburr will be appearing around the Raid host Gym for 30 minutes. Keep in mind that this Raid type is accessible only with Raid Passes and Premium Battle Passes.

    Last but not least, there will be a Community Day map available, where you can find other Trainers, or connect with local Community Ambassadors.
