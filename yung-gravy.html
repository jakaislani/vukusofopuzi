    Who is Yung Gravy?

    Yung Gravy, the actual name Matthew Hauri, was born on March 19, 1996, in Rochester, Minnesota to Cynthia and Swiss-born psychologist Peter. He attended Mayo High School as a teenager, graduating in 2014. Hauri then attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where he got a degree in marketing in 2017. He started rapping in college, influenced by the success of rappers Lil Peep and Lil Yachty. Hauri also had a modest pizza delivery service.
