    Celebrity makeup artist and entrepreneur who owns The Glam Shop hail salon. She also became owner and CEO of Sierra's Secrets Cosmetics. She joined the 6th season of Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta as a supporting player. Her salon clientele has included Zonnique, Toya Wright and Reginae Carter, among others.

    Before Fame

    She became pregnant at the age of 15, leading her life path on an unexpected direction. She was kicked out of her home, and was able to make some money by working at a makeup kiosk in a mall.

    Trivia

    She authored a book called Behind the Glam. 

    Family Life

    She and her husband Rod Gates were together seven years before divorcing in 2018. They had two children: Mason and Sierra.

    Associated With

    One of her employees is Lovely Mimi, a fellow Love & Hip Hop cast member as well.
